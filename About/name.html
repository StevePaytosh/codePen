About

My name is Steve Paytosh, I was born and raised in Lorain, Ohio. Lorain is a modestly sized city not too far away from Cleveland. It is home to a highly diverse poplulation of individuals who's ancestors came from all over the world for find work. These days, that work is gone as the city has become the standard for rust belt towns.

Having seen the strugles of middle class labor first hand has motivated me to acquire not only a college degree, but usefull skills. So I am pursing a degree in Computer Science from the University of Toledo.

As part of my education,I work as a co-op for Ridgid Software Solutions, where I have worked with both web development and big data.

I personally believe that javascript will be the way of the future and that the machines won't be conquering humanity because javascript will have already done it.

I'm open to pretty much all aspects of Computer Science, so until I've set my sights to a unique application of coding, I will let the great digital wind take me wherever it sees fit.

One of the more interesting stops on my journey thus far has been a website dedicated to teaching web development, it's called FreeCodeCamp. They do some amazing work and I highly recommend checking them out.
